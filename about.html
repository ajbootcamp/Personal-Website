About Me

Greetings! My name is April Joy Lintao, and I'm a committed IT student who loves creativity and technology. Information technology is a course that I'm now studying for my degree because I find it fascinating how much possibility this industry has to influence the future.

Apart of the fields of algorithms and code, music is a source of happiness and comfort for me. Since I was young, singing and playing music have been my favorite pastimes. They have given me a way to express myself and an escape from the challenges of everyday life.

Music is a constant source of inspiration and fuel for my soul, whether I'm uttering out a song or just strumming a guitar. I try to create harmony in the vast world of music and technology by integrating my creative inclinations with my analytical abilities.
